Welcome To Your Next Adventure!

Explore


Come make your next adventure in the Mournes. The Mournes are one of Northern Ireland's beautiful areas of outstanding beauty.
You can find the Mournes located in County Down in the southeast corner. This mountain range will leave you breathless.
The Mournes boast Northern Ireland's highest mountain range with Slieve Donard being the highest at 850m.
Having 12 peaks that make up the Mournes means there is something for everyone here.

Visit


With such a large variety of activites, the Mournes are yours to make memories.
Go get lost exploring Annalong woods in the morning,
Go mountain biking in Kilbroney Park for the afternoon,
Then end the day in Rostrevor for a lovely dinner.
The choices are endless!
